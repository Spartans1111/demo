Welcome to AVRIO’s Cloud based LNG-LCHS Course!

We’re thrilled to have you join us on this journey to mastering the complexities of Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) operations. Our course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of LNG processes, safety protocols, and industry best practices, all from the comfort of your own home or office.

Course Focus

The course focuses on ensuring understanding of the principles involved, and the undertaking of all cargo operations carried out on an LNG carrier, in a safe and efficient manner.  

Practical Exercises

Practical exercises structured around the procedures and operation of the cargo and ballast installation of an LNG tanker. 

Operation & Control of Cargo

A safer and more effective contribution to the operation and control of the cargo and ballast installation of a LNG tanker. 

Simulated Cargo Exercises

Simulated cargo exercises conducted using a 'State of the art' LNG Cargo Operations Simulator, covering all subject areas recommended by IMO Model Course 1.36 “Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) Tanker Cargo and Ballast Handling Simulator”.
